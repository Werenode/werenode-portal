PRIVACY POLICY
This privacy policy explains how Werenode SAS (“Werenode,” “we” and “us”) obtains, utilizes,
shares and safeguards information collected from Customers and visitors to its websites
and applications, including without limitation werenode.com (each, a “Website,” collectively,
the “Websites”) and Play Store and Apple Store Apps.
By visiting our Websites or by becoming a Werenode Customer, you consent to the collection,
use and transfer of your personal data pursuant to the terms of this privacy policy.
The Personal Data We Collect From You
Werenode collects personal data that you choose to provide to us on the Websites (including
information referenced in the “The Use of Cookies” section of this Privacy Policy), through email,
over the phone and otherwise, including your name, email address and if you are a potential
Customer, we additionally collect your location if you choose to share it with us for the purpose of
the application (locating nearby charging stations or locating the charging stations that you choose to
share), your card payment information by our partner Stripe API if you choose to pay by card,
cryptocurrency payment preferences and feedback concerning your experience as a Customer.
In addition, Werenode’s Websites and Apps will automatically record information about each
transaction for charging which is made if you are a Customer.

Werenode may place small files called “cookies” on your computer when you visit the Websites.
Cookies facilitate your utilization of the Websites and enable us to track activity and customize
your website experience. If you do not wish to receive a cookie from Werenode SAS, you may
set your Internet browser settings to decline or disable the cookie, or refuse its
installation when prompted.
How Your Personal Data is Used
We utilize personal data collected to allow the Apps and Websites to function properly.
and, in the case of Customers, process your invoices.
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing or solicitation
purposes.
Werenode and trusted third parties utilize personal data only for the purposes for
which it was obtained.
