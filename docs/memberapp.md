# Member App
Welcome to the **Werenode Member App** User Manual. This guide will help you understand and navigate the various features of the Werenode Member App. This app is designed to enhance your experience as a Werenode user, providing detailed information about your charging sessions, monitoring usage, and managing your account with ease.

## Overview
The **Werenode Member App** is built using **Angular** and serves as a central platform for Werenode members to monitor and manage electric vehicle (EV) charging sessions. The app enables users to access detailed session information, track energy usage, manage payments, and configure personal settings—all from one convenient place.

## Key Features
- **Charging Session Overview**: Get real-time and historical data about all your charging sessions, including energy consumed, duration, and cost.
- **User Insights**: Analyze your charging behavior and track your energy usage to optimize charging habits.
- **Account Management**: Update your user profile, view transaction history, and manage charging stations registered under your account.
- **Notifications**: Receive updates on charging status, alerts for completed sessions, and payment confirmations.
- **EVSE Management**: Manage your registered EVSE (Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment) devices.

## Installation

### Requirements
- **Device Compatibility**: The Werenode Member App is available for both **Android** and **iOS** devices.
- **Account**: A valid Werenode account is required to use the app. If you do not have an account, you can register through the app or on the Werenode website.

### Steps to Install
1. **Download the App**:
   - For **Android**: Visit the **Google Play Store** and search for "Werenode Member App."
   - For **iOS**: Visit the **Apple App Store** and search for "Werenode Member App."
2. **Install the App**: Tap on the **Install** button to download and install the app on your device.
3. **Open the App**: Once installed, open the app and log in with your **Werenode account credentials**.

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. **Launch the App**: Open the Werenode Member App on your mobile device.
2. **Enter Credentials**: Enter your **email address** and **password** to log in.
3. **Two-Factor Authentication (Optional)**: If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the code sent to your registered email or mobile number.

### Dashboard Overview
Upon successful login, you will see the **Dashboard**, which provides an overview of your charging activity and account status.
- **Charging Status**: View current and past charging sessions, including **location**, **duration**, and **energy consumption**.
- **Notifications**: Access any important notifications regarding your charging activities.
- **Account Balance**: View your current balance for payments or rewards in **Werecoin (WRC)** or **BWRC** tokens.

## Key Functionalities

### 1. Charging Session Management
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: View real-time information about ongoing charging sessions, including energy usage and estimated completion time.
- **Session History**: Access detailed information on past charging sessions, including:
  - **Location**: Where the session took place.
  - **Energy Consumed**: The total energy used during the session.
  - **Duration**: How long the session lasted.
  - **Cost**: The cost of the charging session, which can be paid using **Werecoin (WRC)** or **BWRC** tokens.

### 2. User Insights
The **User Insights** section provides detailed analytics on your charging habits:
- **Energy Usage**: Track your energy consumption over time and analyze patterns.
- **Optimal Charging Times**: Get recommendations for optimal charging times based on historical data and energy demand.

### 3. Account Management
- **Profile Update**: Edit your personal details such as your **name**, **email address**, and **password**.
- **Transaction History**: View your past payments for charging sessions, including payments made in **WRC** or **BWRC** tokens.
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable or disable two-factor authentication for enhanced account security.

### 4. EVSE Management
The **EVSE Management** section allows users to manage their own Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE):
- **Add New EVSE**: You can register your own charging station to the Werenode network.
  - **Enter EVSE Details**: Provide information such as the **location**, **connector type**, **charging speed**, and **availability**.
  - **Pricing Configuration**: Set pricing for usage (per kWh or per session) and specify public or private availability.
- **Manage Existing EVSE**:
  - **Edit Details**: Update your EVSE information, such as **availability** or **pricing**.
  - **Track Usage**: Monitor how often your charging station is used, view revenue, and assess energy consumption.

### 5. Notifications
The Werenode Member App keeps you updated with relevant notifications:
- **Charging Alerts**: Get notified when your charging session starts, reaches certain milestones, or completes.
- **Payment Confirmations**: Receive notifications when payments have been successfully processed using **Werecoin (WRC)** or **BWRC** tokens.
- **System Updates**: Be informed about system maintenance, new features, and important announcements related to Werenode services.

### 6. Payments and Wallet Integration
The Werenode Member App is integrated with **blockchain-based wallets** to facilitate secure, decentralized payments for charging sessions:
- **Supported Tokens**: Payments can be made using **Werecoin (WRC)** on the **Tezos blockchain** or **BWRC** on the **Binance Smart Chain**.
- **Wallet Integration**:
  - **Connect Your Wallet**: Go to the **Wallet** section and connect your preferred blockchain wallet such as **MetaMask**.
  - **Add Funds**: Fund your wallet with **WRC** or **BWRC** tokens by purchasing them on decentralized exchanges such as **Quipuswap**, **PancakeSwap**, or other supported DEX platforms.
- **Payment Process**:
  - When initiating a charging session, select your preferred token for payment.
  - **Approve the Transaction** through your blockchain wallet to complete the payment.

### 7. User Settings
- **Language Preferences**: Change the app language in the **Settings** section.
- **Notifications Preferences**: Manage your notification settings—enable or disable alerts for charging, payments, and system updates.
- **Privacy and Security**:
  - Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for added security.
  - **Manage Permissions**: Adjust the app's permissions, such as location services, to optimize your experience.

## Troubleshooting and FAQs

### Common Issues
1. **Unable to Start a Charging Session**:
   - **Check Wallet Balance**: Ensure you have enough funds (WRC or BWRC tokens) in your connected wallet.
   - **Network Connection**: Verify that your mobile device has a stable internet connection.
2. **Issues Connecting to EVSE**:
   - **EVSE Availability**: Make sure the EVSE is available and not currently in use by another user.
   - **Update App**: Ensure your app is updated to the latest version for compatibility with the latest features.
3. **Payment Not Going Through**:
   - **Wallet Connectivity**: Check if your blockchain wallet is properly connected.
   - **Gas Fees**: Ensure that you have enough balance to cover network gas fees.

### Frequently Asked Questions
- **How do I find nearby charging stations?**
  - Use the **Map View** within the Dashboard to locate available charging stations nearby. You can filter results by **connector type**, **speed**, and **availability**.
- **What is Werecoin (WRC) and BWRC?**
  - **Werecoin (WRC)** is Werenode’s token on the **Tezos blockchain**, while **BWRC** is the token on the **Binance Smart Chain**. Both tokens are used to facilitate payments for charging sessions.
- **How do I add my own charging station to the Werenode network?**
  - Navigate to **EVSE Management** > **Add New EVSE**, and fill in the details of your charging point. Set your pricing preferences and availability to start offering your station for public or private use.

## Safety and Security Tips
- **Keep Your Account Secure**: Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** to add an extra layer of security.
- **Manage Wallet Safety**: Store your wallet’s private key securely. Do not share it with anyone.
- **Verify Charging Station Information**: Always verify the details of the charging station, such as **location** and **connector type**, before initiating a session.
